Hawaiian meatballs

Description

Whether youâ€™re making them for an easy party appetizer, or serving them as a main dish over rice, Hawaiian Meatballs are sure to be hit! All you need is pineapple, BBQ sauce and meatballs, and you can cook them in the slow cooker or on the stovetop.

Ingredients

  • pineapple
  • BBQ sauce
  • meatballs

How to Make Hawaiian Meatballs:

In the Slow Cooker:

  1. Spray the bottom of your slow cooker lightly with cooking spray. Add frozen meatballs to the bottom of pot.
  2. In a large bowl stir together homemade bbq sauce and crushed pineapple. Pour over meatballs.
  3. Cook on LOW for 4-6 hours or HIGH for 2-3 hours. Serve warm garnished with chopped green onions, if desired.
  4. Add homemade bbq sauce and crushed pineapple to a medium pot.
  5. Add meatballs and stir to coat. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to simmer.
  6. Cook for 8-10 minutes for thawed meatballs, or 14-16 minutes for frozen meatballs.
  7. Serve warm garnished with chopped green onions, if desired.
